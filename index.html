THE FOUNDATION

WHAT IS IT?

Gender equality is the state where gender and individuals attains and being included the same human rights, opportunities, and treatment. It's a fundamental human right essential to achieve peace, full human potential, and sustainable development.

MISSION

Promoting gender equality and empowerment for women and gender-diverse people across the globe.

VISION

A society where all people are treated equally and are free from discrimination.

HISTORY

Gender Equality became part of international law via the Universal Declaration of Human Rights (Dec 10, 1948).

LEGAL & MYTHS

LEGAL PROTECTIONS

SAFE SPACES ACT (RA 11313)

Protects against harassment in public and online.

MAGNA CARTA (RA 9710)

Ensures non-discrimination and health rights.

ANTI-SEXUAL HARASSMENT (RA 7877)

Penalizes harassment in work and education.

MYTH VS. FACT

Myth: Equality is only for women.

Fact: It helps men break toxic masculinity and share roles freely.

Myth: Women get more rights.

Fact: It means equal rights for all, not more for one group.

Myth: Men must be providers.

Fact: Both can work and share family responsibilities.
